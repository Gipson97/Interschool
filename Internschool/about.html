About us

This website has to do with online jobs. You can either sign up as a job seeker or as a "job giver."

The registration to the website is currently free and there is possibility in the near future to provide premium services with a certain fee. As a job seeker you can sign up to the website and then look for jobs and apply to the ones that suit you. As a "job giver" you are able to post jobs to our website.
